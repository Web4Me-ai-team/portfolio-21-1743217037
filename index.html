# About Me

Rachel Green is an accomplished academic with extensive experience in teaching and research in English literature. She has a strong background in literary modernism and Southern literature, with a focus on the works of Faulkner, Wolfe, and Williams. Rachel has a proven track record of integrating multimedia and interdisciplinary approaches in her teaching, and she has contributed significantly to academic publications and conferences.
